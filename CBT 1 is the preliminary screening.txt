RRB NTPC CBT 2 Syllabus
CBT 2 is more detailed and subject-focused. The syllabus is similar to CBT 1 but with a deeper focus and greater difficulty.

General Awareness
Emphasis on recent news and events, especially related to Indian history, geography, and economics.
Further depth in Indian Art and Culture, Polity, and General Science.
Additional topics like budget, important government schemes, and basic scientific concepts.
Mathematics
Topics from CBT 1, but questions are more complex.
More focus on multi-step problem-solving questions in topics like Algebra, Trigonometry, Geometry, and Statistics.
General Intelligence & Reasoning
Advanced puzzles, seating arrangements, and complex data sufficiency.
Greater emphasis on logical reasoning, with multi-layered questions on topics like coding-decoding, syllogism, and analogies.
